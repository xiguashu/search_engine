sonnet xciisonnet xciibut  thy worst  steal thyself away,
 term  life thou art assured mine,
 life  longer  thy love  stay,
  depends   love  thine.
     fear  worst  wrongs,
       life hath end.
  a  state   belongs
    thy humour doth depend;
thou canst  vex   inconstant mind,
   life  thy revolt doth lie.
o,  a happy title   find,
happy   thy love, happy  die!
   blessed-fair  fears  blot?
thou mayst  false,      not.
