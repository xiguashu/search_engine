 flourish. enter king henry vi, clarence, warwick,  somerset, henry  richmond, oxford, montague,  lieutenant   tower  king henry vi  master lieutenant,   god  friends 
  shaken edward   regal seat, 
  turn'd  captive state  liberty, 
  fear  hope,  sorrows  joys, 
   enlargement   thy due fees? 
 lieutenant  subjects  challenge    sovereigns; 
    humble prayer  prevail, 
   crave pardon   majesty. 
 king henry vi   what, lieutenant?    me? 
 nay,  thou    requite thy kindness, 
      imprisonment a pleasure; 
 ay,  a pleasure  incaged birds 
 conceive    moody thoughts 
    notes  household harmony 
   forget  loss  liberty. 
 but, warwick,  god, thou set'st  free, 
  chiefly    god  thee; 
    author, thou  instrument. 
 therefore,    conquer fortune's spite 
  living low,  fortune  hurt me, 
    people   blessed land 
    punish'd   thwarting stars, 
 warwick,   head  wear  crown, 
   resign  government  thee, 
  thou art fortunate   thy deeds. 
 warwick   grace hath   famed  virtuous; 
      wise  virtuous, 
  spying  avoiding fortune's malice, 
   men rightly temper   stars: 
        blame  grace, 
  choosing   clarence   place. 
 clarence  no, warwick, thou art worthy   sway, 
    heavens  thy nativity 
 adjudged  olive branch  laurel crown, 
     blest  peace  war; 
    yield thee  free consent. 
 warwick    choose clarence   protector. 
 king henry vi  warwick  clarence give    hands: 
  join  hands,    hands  hearts, 
   dissension hinder government: 
     protectors   land, 
     lead a private life 
   devotion spend   days, 
  sin's rebuke   creator's praise. 
 warwick   answers clarence   sovereign's will? 
 clarence    consents,  warwick yield consent; 
   thy fortune  repose myself. 
 warwick  why, then,  loath,     content: 
  yoke together,  a double shadow 
  henry's body,  supply  place; 
  mean,  bearing weight  government, 
   enjoys  honour   ease. 
 and, clarence,       needful 
 forthwith  edward  pronounced a traitor, 
    lands  goods  confiscate. 
 clarence   else?   succession  determined. 
 warwick  ay,  clarence     part. 
 king henry vi  but,       chief affairs, 
   entreat,   command  more, 
  margaret  queen   son edward 
   for,  return  france  speed; 
 for,     here,  doubtful fear 
  joy  liberty   eclipsed. 
 clarence     done,  sovereign,   speed. 
 king henry vi   lord  somerset,  youth  that, 
        tender care? 
 somerset   liege,   young henry, earl  richmond. 
 king henry vi   hither, england's hope. 

 lays  hand   head   secret powers 
 suggest  truth   divining thoughts, 
  pretty lad  prove  country's bliss. 
    full  peaceful majesty,

    him,  lords,

 enter a post  warwick   news,  friend? 
 post   edward  escaped   brother,

   lord hastings,  attended

  hunting   daily exercise.
