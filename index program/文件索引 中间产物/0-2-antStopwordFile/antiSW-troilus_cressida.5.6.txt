 vi.  part   plains. troilus  cressida     |  troiles  cressida  |  5,  6 
    |      vi.  part   plains. 
 enter ajax  ajax  troilus, thou coward troilus, show thy head! 

 enter diomedes  diomedes  troilus,  say!  troilus? 
 ajax   wouldst thou? 
 diomedes    correct him. 
 ajax     general, thou shouldst   office 
 ere  correction. troilus,  say! what, troilus! 

 enter troilus  troilus  o traitor diomed! turn thy false face, thou traitor, 
  pay thy life thou owest    horse! 
 diomedes  ha, art thou there? 
 ajax   fight   alone: stand, diomed. 
 diomedes     prize;     upon. 
 troilus  come,   cogging greeks;    both! 

 exeunt, fighting 
 enter hector  hector  yea, troilus? o,  fought,  youngest brother! 

 enter achilles  achilles      thee, ha!   thee, hector! 
 hector  pause,  thou wilt. 
 achilles    disdain thy courtesy, proud trojan: 
  happy   arms    use: 
  rest  negligence befriends thee now, 
  thou anon shalt hear   again; 
  when,  seek thy fortune. 

 exit  hector  fare thee well: 
       a fresher man, 
   expected thee.  now,  brother! 

 re-enter troilus  troilus  ajax hath ta'en aeneas:   be? 
 no,   flame  yonder glorious heaven, 
    carry him:   ta'en too, 
  bring  off: fate, hear    say! 
  reck      life to-day. 

 exit 
 enter   sumptuous armour  hector  stand, stand, thou greek; thou art a goodly mark: 
 no? wilt thou not?   thy armour well; 
  frush   unlock  rivets all, 
    master  it: wilt thou not, 
 beast, abide? 
 why,  fly on,  hunt thee  thy hide. 

 exeunt     |  troiles  cressida  |  5,  6
