 enter valentine  proteus  valentine  cease  persuade,  loving proteus: 
 home-keeping youth   homely wits.

 exit  proteus    honour hunts,   love: 
  leaves  friends  dignify  more,

 thou, julia, thou hast metamorphosed me, 
   neglect  studies, lose  time, 
 war  good counsel, set  world  nought; 
  wit  musing weak, heart sick  thought.
