    fearful  degenerate; 
    revenge  cease  weep. 
    cease  weep    this? 
    head lie   throbbing breast: 
    body    embrace? 
 buckingham   answer   grace   rebels' 
 supplication? 
 king henry vi   send  holy bishop  entreat; 
  god forbid   simple souls 
  perish   sword!   myself, 
   bloody war  cut  short, 
  parley  jack cade  general: 
  stay,  read    again. 
 queen margaret  ah, barbarous villains! hath  lovely face 
 ruled,  a wandering planet,  me, 
     enforce   relent, 
   unworthy  behold  same? 
 king henry vi  lord say, jack cade hath sworn   thy head. 
   ay,   hope  highness   his. 
 king henry vi   now, madam! 
  lamenting  mourning  suffolk's death? 
  fear me, love,      dead, 
 thou wouldst   mourn'd    me. 
 queen margaret  no,  love,    mourn,  die  thee. 

 enter a messenger  king henry vi   now!  news?  comest thou   haste? 
 messenger   rebels   southwark; fly,  lord! 
 jack cade proclaims  lord mortimer, 
 descended   duke  clarence' house, 
  calls  grace usurper openly 
  vows  crown   westminster. 
  army  a ragged multitude 
  hinds  peasants, rude  merciless: 
 sir humphrey stafford  h  brother's death 
 hath   heart  courage  proceed: 
  scholars, lawyers, courtiers, gentlemen, 
  call false caterpillars,  intend  death. 
 king henry vi  o graceless men!      do. 
 buckingham   gracious lord, return  killingworth, 
  a power  raised  put  down. 
 queen margaret  ah,   duke  suffolk  alive, 
  kentish rebels    appeased! 
 king henry vi  lord say,  traitors hate thee; 
      killingworth. 
      grace's person   danger. 
  sight    odious   eyes; 
     city   stay 
  live   secret   may. 

 enter  messenger  messenger  jack cade hath  london bridge: 
  citizens fly  forsake  houses: 
  rascal people, thirsting  prey, 
 join   traitor,   jointly swear 
  spoil  city   royal court. 
 buckingham   linger not,  lord, away,  horse. 
 king henry vi  come, margaret; god,  hope,  succor us. 
 queen margaret   hope  gone,  suffolk  deceased. 
 king henry vi  farewell,  lord: trust   kentish rebels. 
 buckingham  trust nobody,  fear   betray'd. 
    trust      innocence, 
     bold  resolute.
