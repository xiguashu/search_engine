 enter cassio   musicians  cassio  masters, play here;   content  pains; 
   brief;  bid 'good morrow, general.' 

 music 
 enter clown  clown   masters,   instruments   naples, 
   speak i'  nose thus? 
  musician  how, sir, how! 
 clown   these,  pray you, wind-instruments? 
  musician  ay, marry,  they, sir. 
 clown  o,  hangs a tail. 
  musician   hangs a tale, sir? 
 clown  marry. sir,   a wind-instrument   know. 
 but, masters,  money  you:   general 
  likes  music,   desires you,  love's 
 sake,     noise  it. 
  musician  well, sir,   not. 
 clown      music     heard, to't 
 again: but,     hear music  general 
   greatly care. 
  musician     such, sir. 
 clown   put   pipes   bag,   away: 
 go; vanish  air; away! 

 exeunt musicians  cassio  dost thou hear,  honest friend? 
 clown  no,  hear   honest friend;  hear you. 
 cassio  prithee,   thy quillets.  a poor piece 
  gold  thee:   gentlewoman  attends 
  general's wife  stirring,    
  cassio entreats  a  favour  speech: 
 wilt thou  this? 
 clown    stirring, sir:    stir hither,  
    notify  her.
