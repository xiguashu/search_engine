 viii.  part   plains. troilus  cressida     |  troiles  cressida  |  5,  8 
    |      viii.  part   plains. 
 enter hector  hector   putrefied core,  fair without, 
 thy goodly armour  hath cost thy life. 
    day's work done;   good breath: 
 rest, sword; thou hast thy fill  blood  death. 

 puts   helmet  hangs  shield   
 enter achilles  myrmidons  achilles  look, hector,   sun begins  set; 
  ugly night  breathing   heels: 
    vail  darking   sun, 
  close  day up, hector's life  done. 
 hector    unarm'd; forego  vantage, greek. 
 achilles  strike, fellows, strike;      seek. 

 hector falls  so, ilion, fall thou next! now, troy, sink down! 
  lies thy heart, thy sinews,  thy bone. 
 on, myrmidons,  cry   amain, 
 'achilles hath  mighty hector slain.' 

 a retreat sounded  hark! a retire   grecian part. 
 myrmidons   trojan trumpets sound  like,  lord. 
 achilles   dragon wing  night o'erspreads  earth, 
 and, stickler-like,  armies separates. 
  half-supp'd sword,  frankly   fed, 
 pleased   dainty bait,    bed. 

 sheathes  sword  come, tie  body   horse's tail; 
   field    trojan trail. 

 exeunt     |  troiles  cressida  |  5,  8
