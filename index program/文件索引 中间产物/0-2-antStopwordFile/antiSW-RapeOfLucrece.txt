  deep vow,  brutus  before,
 doth  repeat,    swore.
   sworn   advised doom,
  conclude  bear dead lucrece thence;
 show  bleeding body  rome,
   publish tarquin's foul offence:
    speedy diligence,
 romans plausibly  give consent
 tarquin's everlasting banishment.
