 enter sir john somerville

 and,  thy guess,  nigh  clarence now? 


 somerset    southam   leave    forces, 
     expect      hours    . 

 drum heard 
 warwick    clarence   hand,  hear  drum. 

 somerset      his,  lord;  southam lies: 
   drum  honour hears marcheth  warwick. 


 warwick      be? belike, unlook'd-for friends. 

 somerset      hand,    quickly know. 
 march: flourish. enter king edward iv, gloucester,	 soldiers 
 king edward   go, trumpet,   walls,  sound a  parle  . 

 gloucester      surly warwick mans  wall! 

 warwick  o  unbid spite!  sportful edward come? 
   slept  scouts,     seduced, 
     hear  news    repair  ? 


 king edward   now, warwick, wilt thou  ope   city gates, 
 speak  gentle  words  humbly bend thy knee, 
 call  edward king    hands beg mercy? 
     pardon thee  outrages. 


 warwick  nay, rather, wilt thou draw thy forces    , 
 confess   set thee   pluck'd thee own, 
 call  warwick patron   penitent? 
   thou shalt  remain  duke  york. 


 gloucester    thought  ,  least,      king; 
        jest    will? 


 warwick     a dukedom, sir, a goodly gift? 

 gloucester  ay,   faith,  a poor earl  give: 
    thee service   good a gift. 


 warwick  'twas   gave  kingdom  thy brother. 

 king edward      'tis mine,    warwick's gift. 

 warwick  thou  art  atlas   great a weight: 
   weakling, warwick takes  gift again; 
   henry   king, warwick  subject. 


 king edward     warwick's king  edward's prisoner: 
 and, gallant warwick,    answer  this: 
     body   head  off? 


 gloucester  alas,  warwick    forecast, 
 but,  whiles    thought   steal   single  ten, 
   king  slily finger'd    deck  ! 
   left poor henry   bishop's palace, 
 and, ten  one,  meet    tower. 


 edward  'tis  so;    warwick still. 

 gloucester  come, warwick,   time; kneel down, kneel down: 
 nay, when? strike now,    iron cools. 


 warwick      chop  hand   a  blow  , 
      fling   thy face, 
   bear   a sail,  strike  thee. 


 king edward   sail   thou canst,  wind  tide thy  friend  , 
   hand,  fast  wound   thy coal-black  hair 
 shall,  whiles  thy head  warm    cut  off, 
 write    dust  sentence  thy blood, 
 'wind-changing warwick   change  more.' 

 enter oxford,  drum  colours 
 warwick  o  cheerful colours!   oxford comes! 

 oxford  oxford, oxford,  lancaster! 
    forces enter  city 
 gloucester    gates   open  ,   enter too. 

 king edward      foes  set   backs. 
 stand    good array;    doubt 
   issue    bid   battle  : 
   not,  city    small  defence  , 
   quickly  rouse   traitors   same. 


 warwick  o, welcome, oxford!    thy help. 
 enter montague  drum  colours 
 montague  montague, montague,  lancaster! 
    forces enter  city 
 gloucester  thou   thy brother   buy  treason 
     dearest blood  bodies bear. 


 king edward     harder  match  'd,  greater victory: 
   mind presageth happy gain  conquest. 

 enter somerset,  drum  colours 
 somerset  somerset, somerset,  lancaster! 
    forces enter  city 
 gloucester     thy name,  dukes  somerset, 
   sold  lives   house  york; 
   thou shalt      sword hold. 

 enter clarence,  drum  colours 
 warwick    lo,  george  clarence sweeps along, 
   force    bid  brother  battle  ; 
     upright zeal   prevails 
     nature  a brother's love! 
 come, clarence, come; thou wilt,  warwick call. 


 clarence  father   warwick,     means? 
   red rose    hat    here,  throw  infamy  thee 
     ruinate  father's house, 
   gave  blood   lime   stones together, 
   set  lancaster. why,  trow  'st thou, warwick, 
   clarence   harsh,  blunt, unnatural, 
   bend  fatal instruments  war 
    brother   lawful king? 
   thou wilt object  holy oath: 
      oath   impiety 
   jephthah's,   sacrificed  daughter. 
          trespass  
 that,  deserve    brother's hands, 
    proclaim  thy  mortal  foe, 
   resolution, wheresoe'er  meet thee-- 
     meet thee,  thou stir abroad-- 
   plague thee  thy foul misleading me. 
   so, proud-hearted warwick,   defy  thee, 
     brother  turn   blushing cheeks. 
 pardon  me, edward,    amends: 
 and, richard,   frown   faults, 
     henceforth    unconstant. 


 king edward      more,  ten times  beloved, 
    thou  hadst deserved  hate. 


 gloucester  welcome, good clarence;   brotherlike. 

 warwick  o  passing  traitor, perjured   unjust  ! 

 king edward   what, warwick, wilt thou leave  town  fight? 
      beat   stones  thine ears? 


 warwick  alas,    coop'd    defence  ! 
        barnet presently, 
   bid thee  battle  , edward,  thou darest. 


 king edward   yes, warwick, edward dares,  leads  way. 
 lords,   field; saint george  victory!
