 julia    turn not,   return  sooner. 
   remembrance  thy julia's sake. 

 giving a ring  proteus   then,   exchange; here,   this. 
 julia   seal  bargain  a holy kiss. 
 proteus     hand   true constancy; 
    hour o'erslips    day 
   sigh not, julia,  thy sake, 
   ensuing hour  foul mischance 
 torment    love's forgetfulness! 
  father stays  coming; answer not; 
  tide  now: nay,  thy tide  tears; 
  tide  stay  longer   should. 
 julia, farewell!
