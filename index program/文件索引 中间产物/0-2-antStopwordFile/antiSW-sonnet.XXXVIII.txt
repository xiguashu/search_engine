sonnet xxxviiisonnet xxxviiihow   muse  subject  invent,
 thou dost breathe,  pour'st   verse
thine  sweet argument,  excellent
  vulgar paper  rehearse?
o, give thyself  thanks,  aught  
worthy perusal stand  thy sight;
   dumb   write  thee,
 thou thyself dost give invention light?
 thou  tenth muse, ten times   worth
     rhymers invocate;
   calls  thee,   bring 
eternal numbers  outlive long date.
  slight muse    curious days,
 pain  mine,  thine    praise.
